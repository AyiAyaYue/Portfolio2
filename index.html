Mini documentary

Date: 13/06/2021

Role: filming, editing

Team:

For this task we were asked to produce a mini documentary that is no more than 5 minutes. We decided to make an interview for students in our school and asked them to share their experience about studing in Fontys UAS.

We assigned interview questions to ask the candidates which include:

  • Who are they and what is their study?
  • Why did they choose Fontys?
  • Did Fontys meet their expectations?
  • What do they like the most about Fontys?
  • Where do they see themselves in five years?

At the beginning of the video, we used the movement of the character and the changes of background as highlights to attract the audience's attention. To achieve this effect, three identical focal lengths and object distances are required. In order to make the video more entertained and less boring, we took additional footages so as to be interspersed in the interview video.

In some shots, we used an upgraded method in the editing to highlight the beauty of the characters, scenery, and themes. We also tried to use a combination of medium shot, close-up/closes shot to make the video more smooth and vivid.

This video is my first time experience about making a documentary, also my first time to work in a group. Due to a mistake, we lost all the footages when the deadline almost reached out. We had to remake everything in a limited time, from shooting to editing. This further reflects the hard-won of this work.
