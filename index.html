Hello there, I am Yunyue. I am an multimedia designer based in Eindhoven, Netherlands. Currently I am studying in Fontys UAS as a year 1 student.

As a multimedia designer, I focus on front-end development, UX design and media production. I enjoy building up stuffs and make my ideas become real. I am also trying to represent the way I see and sense the world. :) Besides studying, I am a vlogger that mainly record my daily life and my food making process.
